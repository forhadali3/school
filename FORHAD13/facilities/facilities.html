FACILITIES DETAILS
AIS’s mission states that it will provide quality education so that all pupils are able to reach their full potential within a caring and secure environment. This environment is grounded following distinct Support Services, namely

This Library contains fiction and non-fiction books, dictionaries, encyclopedia, revision books for all grades, past papers for all the subjects and religious books.

  1. LIBRARY IS SUPERVISED BY A QUALIFIED LIBRARIAN THROUGHOUT THE SCHOOL TIME.
  2.  STUDENTS FROM GRADE 1-6 ARE HAVING A ‘LIBRARY PERIOD’ ONCE EVERY WEEK WHERE THEY ARE ALLOWED TO READ AND DISCUSS ANY BOOKS OR WATCH EDUCATIONAL PROGRAM ON TV/VIDEO.
  3.  EACH CLASS HAS ONE HOUR A WEEK OF LIBRARY SCIENCE THAT INCLUDES OF INFORMATION LITERACY SKILLS (REFER TO CLASS RULES).

AIS facilitates students and staff with treatment Room having adequate medical supplies and a qualified full-time Nurse and Pharmacist. In case of any sickness/injury Nurse provides first-aid to the student or staff and informs the guardians .

AIS has well-organized Transport system with more than 40 buses and well trained drivers. Teacher assistants are assigned as bus supervisor for all the buses to ensure that each & every child travel safely.

Bookstore contains all the books related to the syllabus & curriculum. Workbooks are compiled by the teachers for all the subjects. In the beginning of Academic Year, Books set are issued to all the students including Textbooks, Notebooks, workbooks, activity books and school diary.

School uniform for all the grades is arranged by the school. School issues the complete uniform sets for summer season, winter season and sports suits.
